# POV: You Suck At Chinese Tones
Yeah thats me. Or at least it was. I was having such a hard time learning tones, but then my teacher did a cool game with me. Where she would play a tone, and I had to guess which one it was. Instanlty I had an idea to just make that into a practice app, and so I did. 

Right after class I spent an hour making it and boom I had my practice app, and then I would just pull it up throughout the day to practice hearing the tones and then repeating them myself so I would get better at my pronunciation. I then posted it on Reddit and peopled loved it! So I decided to keep working on it.
